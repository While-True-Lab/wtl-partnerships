## Usage Notes

- Customize revenue share based on partnership type:

  - Partner-sourced, WTL-delivered: 70% WTL / 30% Partner

- **Always get Aldo's approval** on commercial terms before signing
- Legal review required for deals > IDR 500M or unusual terms
